## Introduction to VLA Robotics

This chapter introduces Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models and explains their importance in robotics.

### Requirements:
- Explain VLA models and their importance.

### Success Criteria:
- Students articulate the VLA pipeline.

---
**Learning Outcomes**:
- Understand the foundational concepts of Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models.
- Explain the significance of integrating vision, language, and action in autonomous robotics.
- Articulate the end-to-end VLA pipeline and its components.
