## Vision-Language Models

This chapter covers scene understanding from vision using Vision-Language Models (VLMs).

### Requirements:
- Scene understanding from vision.

### Success Criteria:
- Robot identifies objects on command.

---
**Learning Outcomes**:
- Understand the architecture and capabilities of Vision-Language Models (VLMs).
- Implement VLMs for robust scene understanding and object identification in robotic systems.
- Enable robots to identify and interact with objects based on natural language commands.
