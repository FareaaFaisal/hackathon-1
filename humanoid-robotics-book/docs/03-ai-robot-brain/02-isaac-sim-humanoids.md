## Isaac Sim for Humanoid Robots

This chapter covers using Isaac Sim for humanoid robots, including scenes, assets, and the USD workflow.

### Requirements:
- Scenes, assets, USD workflow.

### Success Criteria:
- Student loads a humanoid model in Isaac Sim.

---
**Learning Outcomes**:
- Load and manipulate humanoid robot models within Isaac Sim.
- Understand the concept of Universal Scene Description (USD) and its workflow in Isaac Sim.
- Create and manage virtual scenes and assets for humanoid robot simulations.
