## Overview of NVIDIA Isaac Ecosystem

This chapter provides an overview of the NVIDIA Isaac ecosystem, differentiating between Isaac Sim, Isaac SDK, and Isaac ROS.

### Requirements:
- Isaac Sim vs Isaac SDK vs Isaac ROS.

### Success Criteria:
- Students understand ecosystem components.

---
**Learning Outcomes**:
- Differentiate between the various components of the NVIDIA Isaac ecosystem.
- Understand the primary use cases for Isaac Sim, Isaac SDK, and Isaac ROS.
- Identify the appropriate Isaac tool for specific robotics and AI tasks.
