## Navigation for Bipedal Robots

This chapter explores Nav2 concepts and addresses bipedal constraints in navigation.

### Requirements:
- Nav2 concepts.
- Bipedal constraints.

### Success Criteria:
- Robot completes a navigation task.

---
**Learning Outcomes**:
- Understand the core concepts of ROS 2 Navigation (Nav2).
- Address unique challenges and constraints in navigating bipedal robots.
- Implement and test navigation stacks for bipedal systems in simulated environments.
