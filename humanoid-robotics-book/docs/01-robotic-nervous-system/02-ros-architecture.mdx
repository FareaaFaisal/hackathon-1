## ROS 2 Architecture: Nodes, Topics, Services, Actions

This chapter explains DDS, pub/sub, QoS, and uses real examples for humanoid messaging.

### Requirements:
- Explain DDS, pub/sub, QoS.
- Use real examples for humanoid messaging.

### Constraints:
- Use ROS 2 Humble or Iron.

### Success Criteria:
- Student can design a basic ROS graph.

---
**Learning Outcomes**:
- Understand ROS 2 architecture and communication patterns.
- Identify and describe ROS 2 concepts: Nodes, Topics, Services, Actions.
- Apply ROS 2 communication patterns to humanoid robotics scenarios.
