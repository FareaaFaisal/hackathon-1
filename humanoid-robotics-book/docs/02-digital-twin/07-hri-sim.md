## Human-Robot Interaction Sim

This chapter focuses on simulating people, gestures, and obstacles for human-robot interaction.

### Requirements:
- Simulating people, gestures, obstacles.

### Success Criteria:
- Robot interacts with human avatar.

---
**Learning Outcomes**:
- Configure simulations to include human avatars and their interactions with robots.
- Simulate human gestures and complex environmental obstacles.
- Validate robot's ability to safely and effectively interact within a human-populated environment.
