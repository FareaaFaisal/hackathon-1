## Build a Complete Digital Twin

This chapter focuses on combining robot models, sensors, and environments into a complete humanoid twin.

### Requirements:
- Combine robot + sensors + environment.

### Success Criteria:
- Students demonstrate a full humanoid twin.

---
**Learning Outcomes**:
- Integrate all components of a digital twin: robot model, sensors, and environment.
- Troubleshoot and optimize the complete digital twin for performance and accuracy.
- Demonstrate a fully functional digital twin of a humanoid robot in a simulated environment.
