## Physics Simulation (Rigid Bodies, Collisions, Gravity)

This chapter focuses on configuring physics engines and validating humanoid balance simulations.

### Requirements:
- Configure physics engines.
- Validate humanoid balance simulations.

### Success Criteria:
- Accurate collision & inertia behavior.

---
**Learning Outcomes**:
- Configure and fine-tune physics engines for robotic simulations.
- Understand the concepts of rigid bodies, collisions, and gravity in a simulation context.
- Validate the physical accuracy of humanoid robot models, especially concerning balance and interaction.
